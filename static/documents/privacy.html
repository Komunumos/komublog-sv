Privacy Policy

At Komunumos, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users and ensuring the security of their personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our note-taking application that integrates Google Authentication. By using our application, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you use our applications, we may collect certain personal information from you, such as your name, email address, and profile picture associated with your account and/or Google account.

1.2 Application Usage Data

We may also collect non-personal information about your use of the application, such as the notes you create, timestamps, device information, and IP address. This data is used to improve our services and enhance the user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Authentication and Account Management

We use your Google account information to authenticate your identity and provide access to the note-taking application. This enables a seamless login experience without the need to create a separate account.

2.2 Note Synchronization

Your notes and other related data are stored securely and synchronized across devices using your Google account. This ensures that your notes are accessible and up to date whenever you use the application.

2.3 Communication

We may use your email address to communicate with you about important updates, changes to our services, or to respond to your inquiries and support requests.

2.4 Usage Analytics

We may analyze the aggregated and anonymized application usage data to gain insights into how our users interact with the application. This information helps us improve the performance, features, and functionality of our note-taking application.

3. Data Security and Retention

3.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information and prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

3.2 Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. We will securely delete or anonymize your data when it is no longer needed.

4. Information Sharing

4.1 Third-Party Providers

We may share your personal information with trusted third-party service providers that assist us in operating our note-taking application and delivering our services. These providers are contractually obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests, such as subpoenas or court orders.

5. Your Choices and Rights

5.1 Access and Update

You have the right to access, correct, or update your personal information associated with your Google account. You can do this by logging into your Google account and adjusting the relevant settings.

5.2 Opt-Out

You have the option to opt-out of receiving non-essential communications from us. However, please note that we may still send you important administrative messages related to your account and the functioning of the note-taking application.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. We will notify you of any significant changes by email or through a notice within the note-taking application. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@komu.blog.
